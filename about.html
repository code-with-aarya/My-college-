About MJPGP College

MJPGP college

Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Government Polytechnic [MJPGP], established in 2009, is a prestigious Government institution located in Manjhanpur, Uttar Pradesh. The institution is recognized for its academic integrity and future-focused learning approach. It continues to shape professionals through well-structured, industry-aligned programs. Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Government Polytechnic offers 3 diverse courses across multiple disciplines. The institute provides a wide range of specialized programs in Polytechnic, thoughtfully designed to equip students with industry-relevant skills.

Branches
