Principal’s Message

I am proud to lead this institution and work continuously for the overall development of our students. Our aim is to build strong professional ethics, discipline, and practical skills while helping students maintain a balance between their personal and professional lives.

Established in 2009, MJPGP Kaushambi is known for quality technical education and ethical values. Students admitted through JEECUP are among the talented learners of the state.

Our main focus is our students. We encourage disciplined learning, practical knowledge, and skill development. The institute has a good placement record, and our students have performed well in industry, academics, and administration. Along with classroom teaching, we promote laboratory work, industrial training, sports, and cultural activities. I thank all stakeholders for their support in making MJPGP Kaushambi a trusted center of technical education.

Mr. Sujeet Kumar Singh

Principal, MJPGP
